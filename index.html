About Us

Welcome to Mosaique, your gateway to professional opportunities in Japan. Specializing in IT, Mechanical, and Waste Management sectors, we provide comprehensive & dedicated support for individuals aspiring to build their careers in Japan.

Our journey began with an ambition to help professionals succeed in Japan's diverse sectors.Building on our expertise, we embarked on a new mission to support students aspiring to study Japanese and kickstart their careers in Japan.

Mosaique offers comprehensive Japanese language conversation sessions to provide students with essential linguistic and cultural skills. Our services go beyond language acquisition and are led by native speakers with in-depth knowledge of the Japanese job market. From mock interviews to CV writing assistance, we give comprehensive support to ensure your success in obtaining a job in Japan.

Join us at Mosaique and begin your exciting journey to a vibrant career in Japan. Unlock your full potential and let us help you achieve professional success.

At Mosaique, we are proud to offer specialized Japanese language courses for college students. Currently, we provide tailored programs at KPR College and Eshwar College. Our courses are aimed to provide students with the linguistic and cultural expertise required to succeed in the Japanese job market. Join us to improve your language skills and access to intriguing employment prospects in Japan.
